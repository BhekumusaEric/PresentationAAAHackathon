🚨 The Deadly Reality of Mining

12,000+

Mining fatalities worldwide each year

Life-Threatening Hazards

Mining operations face deadly hazards daily: toxic gas leaks, catastrophic rockfalls, machinery accidents, and underground fires that can trap workers with limited escape routes.

Devastating Impact

  • Human Cost: Thousands of preventable deaths and injuries annually
  • Economic Burden: $20+ billion in global losses from mining accidents
  • Operational Impact: Each major incident can halt operations for weeks

Critical Response Gap

Every minute matters in mining emergencies:

  • Current manual reporting systems cause 15-40 minute delays
  • For trapped miners, survival rates drop 50% after the first hour
  • Gas exposure fatalities increase 70% with delayed reporting
